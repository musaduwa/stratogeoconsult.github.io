About Us

StratoGeoConsult is a Nigerian-based consultancy specializing in Geographic Information Systems (GIS), surveying, and project management. We provide innovative and practical geospatial solutions that support development projects, infrastructure planning, and decision- making across various sectors. Our team combines deep technical expertise with field experience to deliver reliable, accurate, and client-centered services.

Vision

To be a leading provider of geospatial and infrastructure consultancy solutions that empower organizations to make informed decisions for sustainable development.

Mission

To deliver cutting-edge GIS services, spatial analytics, and project management solutions that meet the evolving needs of clients in government, academia, and the private sector.

Core Values

Intergrity, Innovation, Excellence, Client Satisfaction, Sustainability
